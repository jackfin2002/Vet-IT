History

Services

Starting as a small card shop in 2002 we here at Rising Dragon Gamers have since grown into a large gaming hotspot boasting many different online, board and card games in venue with a large and growing community, expanding each day. we strive to bring you, the gamer, the best posible gaming experience avaliable too you whether its cards, board games or video game we have everything you need, here at Rising Dragon Gaming, So stop in, have a look, and see what you find.

We offer many services here on site depending on what your looking for. if your more of a cards person, we trade and sell singles along with selling packs and precon decks if your looking to expand your collection, we also have an area set up where you can play against others in our community. We also hold various tournaments weekly for our yugioh, Magic and Vangard player. If your more into board games we sell family board games along with DnD and Warhammer supplies in terms of minis and paints. We also have an area for you and your party to play these game on site. If your interested we also do a DnD podcast every friday afternoon, so if you dont have a party listen in and enjoy our adventures with us. In terms of video games we sell games and gaming equipment. So if you need anything gaming related come down too Rising Dragon Gamers
